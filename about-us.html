ABOUT US:

Purpose of The platform:


  • Allow women to contact or find another woman to contact or find another woman at any time around them that can help get out of an uncomfortable or difficult situation

  • Helps build a community where women travelers can interact with each other and suggest places whicn are safe to visit.

  • Lets contact helpline numbers and get out of a critical situation

OBJECTIVES OF SAFESUPPORT


== SAFETY FOR WOMAN ==

  • Many a times women do have to face a situation where they travel all alone at a scary place or at a place full of dangers. we help the women to contact nearby womens or helpline numbers in order to remain safe.

    • == SAFE WORKING CULTURES, STRONGER COMMUNITIES ==

  • We intend to build a community where a female is able to meet and interact another female at any time and sharing her experience of a particular place visited

    • == ZERO TOLLERANCE OF HARRASSMENT ==

  • In case of any harrasment A female is given an SOS button on the main page in order to contact nearest police stations

    • GOALS:


  • To stop harrasment

  • To maintain society

  • To build up a strong women community
